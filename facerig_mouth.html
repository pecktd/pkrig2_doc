Bases: pkrig2.rig_base.BaseRig

MouthRig module.

Surface’s spans U has to be devisible by 4. Surface’s spans V has to be 1. Both U and V degrees have to be 1. U 0 is at the left corner and loops clockwise.

The number of detail joints is doubled from span U.

Template joints for detail joints are needed. The amount of template joints is the number of span U.
