pkrig2.facerig_eye.generate_lid_joints_on_cvs(lid_curve, eyeball_jnt, along_axis)¶

Gerates eyelid joints to the positions of CVs of the given curve. If along_axis is True, root joints are positioned along the x axis of the given eyeball_jnt else root joitns are positioned at the position of the given eyball_jnt.

Parameters:
  • lid_curve (prc.Dag) –
  • eyeball_jnt (prc.Dag) –
  • along_axis (bool) – Root joint is placed along the X axis of the eyeball_jnt is this is True else is place at the position of the eyeball_jnt.
Returns:

A list of root joints(prc.Joint). list: A list of tip joins(prc.Joint).
