facerig_eyebrow¶

class pkrig2.facerig_eyebrow.EyebrowRig(head_tmpjnt, mid_ctrl_tmpjnt, mid_det_tmpjnts, l_main_tmpjnt, l_tmpjnts, l_det_tmpjnts, r_main_tmpjnt, r_tmpjnts, r_det_tmpjnts, ctrl_tmpcrv, mid_ctrl_tmpcrv, det_ctrl_tmpcrv, desc, ctrl_grp_parent, still_grp_parent, skin_parent)¶

Bases: pkrig2.rig_base.BaseRig

EyebrowRig module.

Place template joints with the concept of Up/Down Out/In the Z axis of the right side joints are inverted so they will be flipped by the script.

Bind the middle curve to the main left and right joints.

Bind the sub control curve to the main left/right joints and the middle joint.

Bind the detail curve to all sub joints and the middle joint.
